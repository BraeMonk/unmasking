Field survey · not a diagnosis

Map where you mask,
not what you are.

This is a progress tracker for unmasking, built around a 28-item self-reflection across seven domains — social scripting, sensory suppression, stimming, eye contact, interests, emotional labor, and executive fatigue.

Take the full map every few weeks to see your terrain shift, and use quick daily logs in between — the way you'd track weight or habits, but for how much you're masking. Answer for how you actually operate, not how you wish you operated.
